Stayed here for a night in their executive suite. The room is big, well furnished and the small balcony has a nice view of the river. However, at 7,000 BDT it is very expensive for a hotel in Mymensingh. The bathroom lights were not working properly and the water heater took hours to warm up. The room itself is good with clean furniture and sofas. Be aware of mosquitoes - better sleep with the lights on! We wanted to eat lunch at the restaurant but they did not have anything other than pizza. It was lunch time and a restaurant should have food to serve. We had to go out for local food. The hotel seemed to be undergoing repairs and extension work. There is a lot that can be done to make this place more attractive. It's location of right on the river bank is a huge bonus. The view from the balcony in the early morning and the fresh air is amazing. by Titu Owner of e-Business

Hotel at first appears to be OK although not for everyone taste with plenty of gaudy items in the garden. Bedroom was very good and food excellent. However the staff does not know the meaning of the word 'work'. Breakfast took a very long time as the young waiter would bring various items one at a time. In the reception at around 9 there was nobody at the desk but a man asleep on the floor. More disturbing for me were two monkeys locked up in a cage about 2 meters wide and deep by about 3 high. These poor creatures were also collared with a chain inside their prison with only a wooden box on the floor for shelter, I suppose and a container of water. I saw people including staff kicking the cage and the animals react in a very aggressive way. There is another cage with a very large bird who could not stretch his wings because of the small size of the cage he is in. By HR Hanif from Bangladesh(mymensingh)
